Getting Started

How do I get started with Forge108?

Getting started is simple. Book a free consultation through our contact page and we'll schedule a call to learn about your brand, your goals, and your current social presence. From there, we'll build a custom strategy and walk you through exactly how we'll help you grow.

What's included in the free consultation?

Our free consultation is a genuine strategy call — no pressure, no sales pitch. We'll review your current social accounts, identify your biggest growth opportunities, discuss your target audience and competitive landscape, and outline how Forge108 can help. It's around 30–45 minutes and entirely no-obligation.

Do you require long-term contracts?

We offer both month-to-month and longer-term engagements. While we recommend a minimum of 3 months to see meaningful, sustainable results, we don't lock clients into contracts they're not comfortable with. We earn your continued business through results.

Services & Process

Which platforms do you manage?

We manage all major social platforms including Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, X (Twitter), YouTube Shorts, and Facebook. Most clients focus on 2–3 platforms where their audience is most active. We'll help you identify the right channels during your strategy session.

Do you create the content or do we?

We handle creative direction, copy, graphics, and scheduling. For video content, we work with footage you send us — we don't do on-location recording, but we produce, edit, and format everything from the raw clips you provide. Think of us as your remote production and strategy team: you capture the moments, we turn them into content that performs.

How do you measure success?

We track what actually matters: follower growth, engagement rate, reach, profile visits, website clicks, and conversion metrics tied to your specific goals. You'll receive a clear monthly report with real numbers — not vanity metrics — along with commentary on what's working and what we're adjusting.

How involved do I need to be?

As involved as you'd like. Many clients prefer a fully hands-off experience — we handle everything and check in monthly. Others like to be more closely involved in content review and strategy. We build our workflow around your preferences so you can focus on running your business.

Results & Pricing

How long until I see results?

Most clients see meaningful engagement improvements within the first 4–6 weeks. Significant audience growth typically follows at the 2–3 month mark as the algorithm recognizes consistent, quality content. Social media is a long-term investment — we'll set honest expectations from day one and keep you informed every step of the way.

Can you work with small businesses or early-stage brands?

Absolutely. We work with brands at every stage — from founders just launching their first social presence to established companies scaling past their growth ceiling. Our strategies are tailored to your current stage and budget, and we're transparent about what's realistic at each level.

How is pricing structured?

Pricing depends on the scope of services, number of platforms, and content volume. We offer tiered packages as well as custom engagements for brands with specific needs. All pricing is discussed openly during your consultation — no hidden fees, no surprises. Book a call to get a tailored quote.
